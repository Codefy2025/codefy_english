About me

A little about me: First name: Niklas. Age: 32. Living in Denmark

Hello. Welcome to my little space of the internet! As a former student of construction management and planning, financial controlling and now computer science, I have now decided to share my wisdom with you all. I am - in my own words - a bit of a tech geek and "left-handed", meaning I can find solutions for problems that others have not throught of through the use of technology. All thanks to my varied knowledge - I am a jack of all trades, master of none kind of guy.

My goals with this site are to:

  • Hopefully help you all with advice and/or solutions to various software development problems
  • To inspire you with ideas for your next side projects
  • To help newcomers into the world of it and software development
  • To provide my knowledge for free (no more pesky paywalls! Through I'll always appreciate a share and/or a donation!)
  • To challenge myself meaning creating something that can be shared with the world and being useful for a lot of people

So are you ready? Then check out my projects. I will continuously add new content and edit existing content.
